CAREERS


HEAD BAKER

  • Locations: Downtown
  • Job Description: Head Baker must complete duties such as adhering to food hygiene standards, coordinating bakery staff, taking charge of customer complaints, ordering ingredients and supplies, and directing various processes, from baking to decorating. Head baker must arrive promptly at 5am.
  • Qualifications: 2 years as Head baker OR 8 years as Baking Associate.
  • Compensation: $45,000-&50,000 salaried



BAKING ASSOCIATE

  • Locations: Downtown, Midtown and Uptown
  • Job Description: Baking Associate assists head baker in all baking and decorating duties, baking associate must arrive promptly at 5am. Job requires regular lifting of 50lbs bags.
  • Qualifications: Prior experience preferred but not required
  • Compensation: $14-$20 an hour (based on experience)



DELIVERY DRIVER

  • Locations: ALL LOCATIONS
  • Job Description: Deliver catering to businesses and events. Regular Lifting of 20lbs
  • Qualifications: Prior experience preferred but not required
  • Compensation: $14 an hour plus tips



CASHIER

  • Locations: ALL LOCATIONS
  • Job Description: Cashier fills orders at bakery counter and takes customer payment. Cashiers are also expected to maintain proper sanitation duties in their work area.
  • Qualifications: Prior experience preferred but not required
  • Compensation: $14 an hour
